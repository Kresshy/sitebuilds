tips & tricks

Tap a blank
area of the screen
to deselect all
selected objects.

When selecting logos
or text styles, slide
your finger across
them to flip through
the many options.

Tap the screen
with two fingers to
hide the tool box.
And again to
bring it back.

Rotate objects by
touching each side of
the object with your
index fingers.
Then rotate.

Double tap
an object to
flip it horizontally.
